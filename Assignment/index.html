Welcome to Xewkija!



Below is a some information about Xewkija

1

Xewkija is an administrative unit of Malta, on the island of Gozo. The population of Xewkija is 3,300 as of March 2014.

2

The name Xewkija is derived from the Maltese word "Xewk", meaning "thistles" or "thorns"

3

Xewkija is famous for its church, The Rotunda of Xewkija, which is dedicated to St. John the Baptist.
